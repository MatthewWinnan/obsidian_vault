# Final Push — Thursday 30 & Friday 31 July

Moving date: **Saturday 1 August 2026** — handover same day
These are your two days off. The goal is to arrive at Saturday morning calm, organised, and ready.

> By the end of Thursday everything should be packed.
> Friday is for closing out, defrosting, and preparing for the day itself.

---

## Thursday 30 July — Pack Everything Remaining

### Morning

#### PCs — Pack Last Active Equipment
- [ ] Back up anything critical before shutting down
- [ ] Shut both PCs down cleanly
- [ ] Photograph cable setups at the back of each PC before unplugging anything
- [ ] Disconnect and bundle all cables per PC — label each bundle
- [ ] Pack PC towers — wrap in moving blanket, transport upright; these go in your car not the truck
- [ ] Any remaining peripherals still on desks — pack now

#### Kitchen — Final Items
- [ ] Pack everything left in the kitchen except the "last morning kit":
  - Remaining pantry items — seal open packets in zip-lock bags, box up
  - Dish soap, sponge, drying rack
  - Any remaining appliances or gadgets missed last weekend
- [ ] **Leave out and set aside in one spot:**
  - Kettle + 2 mugs
  - 1 pot + 1 pan
  - Basic cutlery for 2
  - Coffee/tea + sugar
  - Dish soap + sponge (1)

#### Bathrooms — Final Pack
- [ ] Pack all remaining bathroom items — leave out only:
  - One toiletry bag per person (toothbrush, toothpaste, deodorant, face wash, medications)
  - Toilet paper — leave 1 roll per bathroom
  - Hand soap — 1 per bathroom
- [ ] Pack towels (any remaining) — use as padding around fragile items or box separately
- [ ] Check cabinets, drawers, shower caddies, behind the toilet — easy places to miss things

#### Main Bedroom — Final Clothes
- [ ] Pack all remaining clothes except what you'll wear Thursday, Friday, and moving day
- [ ] Pack all remaining shoes except daily pair
- [ ] Clear and pack bedside items — books, chargers, glasses, anything on top of or in the side tables
- [ ] Strip and pack all bedding except the set you'll sleep on Thursday night
- [ ] Leave pillows and one set of bedding accessible for the last night

### Afternoon

#### Full House Walkthrough — Packing Check
Go room by room and check every single space:
- [ ] Every cupboard and wardrobe — open every door, check every shelf
- [ ] Under every bed
- [ ] Every drawer in every piece of furniture
- [ ] Tops of wardrobes and high shelves
- [ ] Linen closets, broom cupboards, utility spaces
- [ ] Garage shelves and corners
- [ ] Porch — anything still loose outside

#### House Restoration — Final Check
- [ ] Walk every wall in every room — check all holes are filled and sanded
- [ ] All blinds re-hung in correct rooms — pull each one up and down to confirm working
- [ ] All fixtures reattached — hooks, rails, towel bars, toilet roll holders, brackets
- [ ] Touch up any paint that still looks uneven — give it the rest of the day to dry

#### Cat Setup
- [ ] Choose the safe room for moving day (e.g. bathroom or spare room)
- [ ] Set it up now: carrier (door open), familiar blanket, food and water bowls, litter tray, favourite toy
- [ ] Move the Feliway diffuser into this room if it isn't already there

### Evening
- [ ] Do a final box count and confirm all boxes are labelled with room and contents
- [ ] Stack all packed boxes in their respective rooms or in the garage/entrance hall for easy loading
- [ ] Confirm all furniture that needs disassembly is disassembled
- [ ] Charge all devices overnight — phones, laptops, power banks
- [ ] Eat down whatever is left in the fridge for dinner — clear it as much as possible
- [ ] **Unplug the fridge and deep freeze tonight** — doors open, towels on the floor to catch water — they need the full night to defrost

---

## Friday 31 July — Close Out, Defrost, Final Prep

### Morning

#### Defrost + Clean Appliances
- [ ] Fridge — mop up all water, wipe interior dry, leave door open to air out
- [ ] Deep freeze — mop up all water, wipe interior dry, leave door open to air out
- [ ] Both should be completely dry and clean before loading onto the truck Saturday
- [ ] Dishwasher — run one final empty clean cycle, disconnect water inlet and drain hose, tape door shut
- [ ] Tumble dryer — confirm drum is secured if needed for transport

#### First Night Box — Pack This Now
This box/bag travels in your car, not the truck. You will need it the moment you arrive.
- [ ] Toiletries per person (toothbrush, toothpaste, deodorant, face wash, shower gel)
- [ ] 2 days of clothing per person
- [ ] One full set of bedding (sheets, duvet, pillows)
- [ ] Phone chargers + laptop charger + power bank
- [ ] Kettle, 2 mugs, coffee/tea, sugar, teaspoon
- [ ] Snacks and easy food for moving day evening
- [ ] Toilet paper (1 roll)
- [ ] Hand soap (travel size or small bottle)
- [ ] Important documents — IDs, lease agreement, insurance docs, vehicle papers
- [ ] Medications (any prescription or daily meds)
- [ ] Cat essentials — food (1 day), water bowl, small litter tray and litter, familiar toy or blanket
- [ ] Small tool kit — screwdriver, allan keys for reassembling furniture at the new place
- [ ] Label this box/bag clearly: **FIRST NIGHT — DO NOT LOAD ON TRUCK**

#### Router + Network — Pack Last
- [ ] Shut down router and switch cleanly
- [ ] Disconnect all cables — keep router, switch, ONT, and all patch cables together in one clearly labelled bag
- [ ] This goes in your car, not the truck — you want internet up as fast as possible at the new place

### Midday

#### Final Full Walkthrough
Go through every single room one last time with fresh eyes:
- [ ] Is every box sealed and labelled?
- [ ] Is every piece of furniture either packed, disassembled, or wrapped?
- [ ] Are all wall fixtures back in place?
- [ ] Are all blinds up?
- [ ] Are all holes filled?
- [ ] Are there any items missed, forgotten, or still loose?
- [ ] Check outside: porch, garden, garage — nothing left behind

#### Keys
- [ ] Round up all keys: front door (all copies), gate, garage, postbox, any padlocks
- [ ] Put them all in one place — envelope or zip-lock bag labelled "Keys to hand over"

#### Confirm Movers
- [ ] Call movers to reconfirm: arrival time tomorrow morning, your current address, new address
- [ ] Confirm they have your cell number and know to call when 10 minutes away
- [ ] Confirm payment arrangement

### Afternoon / Evening

#### Last Night
- [ ] Sleep on the stripped-down bed with the bedding set aside from Thursday
- [ ] Keep cat in normal routine — don't put them in the safe room until tomorrow morning
- [ ] Do not unplug the fridge or freezer tonight — they're already off, just leave doors open to dry
- [ ] Set an early alarm — movers arriving early means an early start

#### Final Reminders for Tomorrow Morning
- [ ] Cat into safe room first thing, before movers arrive — food, water, litter, door shut, note on door
- [ ] Do not feed cat 2–3 hours before the car journey
- [ ] First Night Box and router bag go in **your car** — point these out to yourself before the truck arrives
- [ ] PC towers and monitors go in **your car** — do not let movers load these on the truck
- [ ] Walk movers through the house before they start loading — explain the label system and flag fragile boxes
