# Week 4 Action Plan (21–27 July)

Moving date: **Saturday 1 August 2026** (handover same day)
Week 4 focus: **Final packing — kitchen, main bedroom, furniture disassembly, and house restoration**

> After this weekend, the house should be almost entirely packed. Only daily-use essentials remain for the final push days (Thu 30 – Fri 31).

---

## Weekdays (Mon 21 – Fri 25 July)
> Food clear-out, final confirmations, and finishing house restoration on evenings.

### Fridge + Freezer — Final Clear
- [ ] Stop buying anything that won't be consumed before 31 July
- [ ] Actively plan every meal around what's left — fridge and freezer should be nearly empty by end of this week
- [ ] Donate or give away anything you won't get through — neighbours, friends, food bank
- [ ] Fridge and deep freeze need to be empty and defrosting by Thursday 30 July night

### Movers — Final Reconfirmation
- [ ] Call or message movers to reconfirm: arrival time on 1 August, both addresses, truck size, number of people
- [ ] Flag anything that's changed since booking: extra items, access issues, new place parking
- [ ] Confirm payment method and amount

### House Restoration — Evenings This Week
- [ ] Continue filling holes in any rooms not done last weekend (main bedroom, gaming area, kitchen, bathrooms)
- [ ] Sand down dried filler from last weekend — check if touch-up paint is needed
- [ ] Touch up paint wherever needed — do early in the week so it has time to fully dry before handover
- [ ] Retrieve blinds from spare room cupboard — make a list of which blind goes in which room

### Supplies Check
- [ ] Confirm enough boxes remain for kitchen (crockery, appliances, pantry) — kitchen typically needs 15–25 boxes
- [ ] Make sure you have plenty of bubble wrap and packing paper for crockery
- [ ] Confirm bin bags for remaining clothing

---

## Weekend (Sat 26 – Sun 27 July)
> Final big packing weekend. After this, only daily essentials remain.

### Saturday — Kitchen + House Restoration

#### Kitchen
- [ ] **Small appliances — pack all of these:**
  - Air fryer — remove basket, wrap both separately
  - Pressure cooker — remove insert and seal, pack separately
  - Toaster — shake out crumbs, wrap and box
  - Snackwich maker — clean plates, wrap and box
  - Microwave — secure door with tape, wrap in moving blanket
  - Any other gadgets, blenders, coffee machines
- [ ] **Crockery and glassware:**
  - Wrap every plate and bowl individually in packing paper or tea towels
  - Pack plates standing upright (less breakage than flat stacking)
  - Wrap cups and mugs individually, pack upright
  - Label all boxes: **FRAGILE / THIS SIDE UP**
- [ ] **Utensils and cutlery** — bundle in zip-lock bags or rubber band, box together
- [ ] **Tupperware** — nest containers, put lids in a zip-lock bag inside the same box
- [ ] **Pantry dry goods** — seal open packets in zip-lock bags before boxing, pack in medium boxes
- [ ] **Condiments and sauces** — pack upright in a box lined with a bin bag in case of leaks
- [ ] **Leave out until final push:**
  - Kettle + 2 mugs
  - 1 pot + 1 pan
  - Basic cutlery for 2
  - Dish soap + sponge
  - Coffee/tea + sugar

#### House Restoration
- [ ] **Re-hang all blinds** — work room by room, refer to your list of which blind belongs where
- [ ] **Reattach all removed fixtures** — go room by room with your week 1 inspection notes: hooks, towel rails, curtain brackets, toilet roll holders, picture rails
- [ ] Do a walkthrough to confirm: every window has its blind, every wall has its fixture back
- [ ] Final check on all filled holes — sand any rough patches, touch up paint if still needed

### Sunday — Main Bedroom + Furniture Disassembly + Porch

#### Main Bedroom
- [ ] **Remaining clothes** — pack everything except 4–5 days of daily wear; use bin bags for clothes still on hangers
- [ ] **Remaining shoes** — box up all except daily pair
- [ ] **Bed frame** — disassemble, bag all bolts and screws, tape the bag to the headboard or frame
- [ ] **Headboard** — wrap in moving blanket or bubble wrap
- [ ] **Dressing table** — if not already disassembled, do so now; wrap mirror if attached, bag all hardware
- [ ] **Side tables** — if not already packed, do now

#### Furniture Disassembly
- [ ] **Gaming area desks** — now that PCs are the only thing left on them, disassemble both; bag all hardware per desk; wrap table tops in moving blankets
- [ ] **Living room** — if not done last weekend: remove table legs, disassemble any remaining shelving, wrap couch sections in moving blankets
- [ ] **Cat post** — wrap base, set aside accessible for moving day

#### Porch
- [ ] Wrap chair and table set — cling wrap or moving blankets, stack chairs if possible
- [ ] Confirm braai and porch bed are gone — if not, arrange final collection or skip hire this week

#### Bathrooms — Light Pack
- [ ] Begin clearing bathroom cabinets — discard expired products, old medicines, anything not worth moving
- [ ] Leave out: daily toiletries, toilet paper (full roll per bathroom), hand soap
- [ ] Everything else into a box or toiletry bag per person — leave accessible, not taped shut yet

---

## End of Week 4 Goals
By Sunday evening you should have:
- [ ] Kitchen fully packed except "last morning kit"
- [ ] Main bedroom fully packed except 4–5 days of clothes and daily shoes
- [ ] Both desks disassembled and wrapped
- [ ] All furniture prepped and wrapped for moving day
- [ ] All blinds re-hung in correct rooms
- [ ] All removed fixtures reattached
- [ ] All holes filled, sanded, and painted
- [ ] Porch packed and cleared
- [ ] Bathrooms 80% cleared
- [ ] Fridge and freezer nearly empty and ready to defrost Thursday night
