# Week 4 Action Plan (21–27 July)

Moving date: **Friday 31 July 2026** — must vacate by 12:00 on Saturday 1 August
Week 4 focus: **Final packing — kitchen, LEGOs, gaming area, main bedroom, furniture disassembly, and house restoration**

> After this weekend, the house should be almost entirely packed. Only daily-use essentials remain for the final push days (Wed 29 – Thu 30).

---

## Weekdays (Mon 21 – Fri 25 July)
> Food clear-out, final confirmations, and finishing house restoration on evenings.

### Fridge + Freezer — Final Clear
- [ ] Stop buying anything that won't be consumed before 31 July
- [ ] Actively plan every meal around what's left
- [ ] Donate or give away anything you won't get through — neighbours, friends, food bank
- [ ] **Fridge must be empty by Monday/Tuesday 28 July** — it gets unplugged the evening of Wednesday 29 July
- [ ] Deep freeze: empty by 28 July too — it gets unplugged the evening of 29 July and moved outside to defrost overnight (drains naturally, no indoor mess, ready for movers to load directly)

### Movers — Final Reconfirmation
- [ ] Call or message movers to reconfirm: arrival time on 31 July, both addresses, truck size, number of people
- [ ] Flag anything that's changed since booking: extra items, access issues, new place parking
- [ ] Confirm payment method and amount

### House Restoration — Evenings This Week
- [ ] Continue filling holes in any rooms not done last weekend (main bedroom, gaming area, kitchen, bathrooms)
- [ ] Sand down dried filler from last weekend — check if touch-up paint is needed
- [ ] Touch up paint wherever needed — do early in the week so it has time to fully dry before handover

### Blinds and Curtains
> New tenants do not want them re-hung — they will remove them anyway.
- [ ] Take all blinds and curtains to be cleaned during the week
- [ ] Once returned, pack them neatly in the spare room (already cleared)

### Supplies Check
- [ ] Confirm enough boxes remain for kitchen (crockery, appliances, pantry) — kitchen typically needs 15–25 boxes
- [ ] Make sure you have plenty of bubble wrap and packing paper for crockery
- [ ] Confirm bin bags for remaining clothing

---

## Weekend (Sat 26 – Sun 27 July)
> Final big packing weekend. After this, only daily essentials remain.

### Saturday — Kitchen Fragiles + House Restoration + LEGOs Start

#### Kitchen
> Non-essential appliances, pantry, and tupperware packed in Week 3 Sunday. Saturday is fragiles and remaining items only.
- [ ] **Air fryer** — remove basket, wrap both separately and box (last appliance out)
- [ ] **Any remaining gadgets** missed in Week 3
- [ ] **Crockery and glassware:**
  - Wrap every plate and bowl individually in packing paper or tea towels
  - Pack plates standing upright (less breakage than flat stacking)
  - Wrap cups and mugs individually, pack upright
  - Label all boxes: **FRAGILE / THIS SIDE UP**
- [ ] **Utensils and cutlery** — bundle in zip-lock bags or rubber band, box together
- [ ] **Tupperware** — nest containers, put lids in a zip-lock bag inside the same box
- [ ] **Remaining pantry dry goods** — anything not boxed in Week 3
- [ ] **Condiments and sauces** — pack upright in a box lined with a bin bag in case of leaks
- [ ] **Leave out until final push:**
  - Kettle + 2 mugs
  - 1 pot + 1 pan
  - Basic cutlery for 2
  - Dish soap + sponge
  - Coffee/tea + sugar

#### House Restoration
- [ ] **Reattach all removed fixtures** — go room by room with your week 1 inspection notes: hooks, towel rails, brackets, toilet roll holders, picture rails
- [ ] Final check on all filled holes — sand any rough patches, touch up paint if still needed
- [ ] Confirm blinds and curtains are cleaned and packed neatly in spare room

#### LEGOs — Start (split across both days)
- [ ] All loose/unbuilt sets first — confirm boxes are sealed and labelled
- [ ] Begin cling wrapping built sets — work through as many as time allows after kitchen and restoration are done

### Sunday — Gaming Area + Main Bedroom + Furniture + Porch + Bathrooms

#### Gaming Area — LEGOs Finish + Pack Everything
- [ ] **LEGOs — finish** ⚠️ remaining built sets from Saturday; cling wrap individually, pack in sturdy boxes with padding, FRAGILE / THIS SIDE UP
- [ ] **Photograph all cable setups** at both PCs before touching anything
- [ ] **Peripherals** — keyboards, mice, headsets, controllers, webcams; bundle cables per device, label clearly
- [ ] **Monitors** — bubble wrap then moving blanket, upright, FRAGILE — set aside to go in your car on moving day
- [ ] **Shelf** — empty, disassemble, wrap boards, bag hardware
- [ ] **Clear the desk area** — everything off the desks except the active PC setup itself
- [ ] **Cat post** — wrap base, set aside accessible for moving day
- [ ] **PCs** — do not pack yet, leave running; pack on final push day Wed 29 July
- [ ] **Desks** — leave in place for now; disassemble Wed 29 July once PCs are packed

#### Main Bedroom
- [ ] **Remaining clothes** — pack everything except 4–5 days of daily wear; use bin bags for clothes still on hangers
- [ ] **Remaining shoes** — box up all except daily pair
- [ ] **Bed frame** — disassemble, bag all bolts and screws, tape the bag to the headboard or frame
- [ ] **Headboard** — wrap in moving blanket or bubble wrap
- [ ] **Dressing table** — if not already done from Week 3; wrap mirror if attached, bag all hardware
- [ ] **Side tables** — confirm done from Week 3, or finish now

#### Living Room — Furniture
- [ ] **Living room** — if not done last weekend: remove table legs, disassemble any remaining shelving, confirm couch is wrapped in moving blankets

#### Porch
- [ ] Wrap chair and table set — cling wrap or moving blankets, stack chairs if possible
- [x] Confirm braai and porch bed are gone

#### Bathrooms — Light Pack
- [ ] Begin clearing bathroom cabinets — discard expired products, old medicines, anything not worth moving
- [ ] Leave out: daily toiletries, toilet paper (full roll per bathroom), hand soap
- [ ] Everything else into a box or toiletry bag per person — leave accessible, not taped shut yet

- [ ] Gaming area fully packed (except PCs and desks — these go on Wed 29 July)
