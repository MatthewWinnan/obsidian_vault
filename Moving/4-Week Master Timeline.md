# 4-Week Master Timeline

Moving date: **Saturday 1 August 2026** — handover same day
Today: 1 July 2026 — 31 days out

> Physical tasks (packing, purging, disassembly) are **weekend only**.
> Weekday tasks are **planning, admin, and arrangements only**.

---

## Week 1 — 1 to 6 July | 4 Weeks Out
**Theme: Decisions, arrangements, first purge passes**

### Weekdays (Tue 1 – Fri 4)
- [ ] Confirm when current occupants vacate new place + handover time on 1 Aug
- [ ] Decide: hire movers vs self-move — get 2–3 quotes, confirm end-of-July availability
- [ ] Set moving budget
- [ ] Arrange packing supplies — order or plan to collect this weekend (boxes, tape, bubble wrap, markers, bin bags, cling wrap, zip-locks, coloured tape)
- [ ] List items for sale online (Facebook Marketplace / Gumtree): fans ×3, braai, porch bed, standing light, soda machine
- [ ] Buy **Feliway diffuser** — plug in now to start calming the cat ahead of the move
- [ ] Put cat carrier out in a visible familiar spot — leave it open with a blanket inside
- [ ] Walk every room with a notepad: log all holes to fill, fixtures to re-attach, windows missing blinds

### Weekend (Sat 5 – Sun 6)
- [ ] Collect / buy all packing supplies
- [ ] **Garage**: full purge pass — sort usable boxes from junk, bag donations/trash, tidy the space
- [ ] **Porch**: purge pass — move braai and porch bed to visible spot for buyers, clean chair/table set, bin loose junk
- [ ] **Spare room**: open the cupboard, start a donation bag for old clothes and unwanted items

## Week 2 — 7 to 13 July | 3 Weeks Out
**Theme: Early disassembly + start packing non-essential areas**

See [[Moving/Week 2 Action Plan|Week 2 Action Plan]] for full detail.

### Weekdays (Mon 7 – Fri 11)
- [ ] Confirm mover booking if not locked in
- [ ] Arrange ISP reconnection at new address — confirm date (aim for 1 Aug or earlier)
- [ ] Arrange electricity/water transfer or new account at new address
- [ ] Coordinate first buyer collections for sale items listed last week
- [ ] Draft full address change notification list: bank, employer HR, medical aid, insurance, subscriptions, Home Affairs, post office

### Weekend (Sat 12 – Sun 13)
- [ ] **Study — Network**: remove all ethernet trunking from walls around the house (can live with exposed cables), unmount router and switch from table and place on floor/shelf — internet stays up
- [ ] **Study — Home Assistant**: shut down cleanly, disassemble, pack HA hardware together in a labelled bag — stability testing restarts at new place
- [ ] **Study — Electronics cupboard**: purge pass, then pack shelves 1–3 into labelled boxes (non-daily-use gear first)
- [ ] **Spare room**: finish packing — confirm Funkos sealed, check book chest weight, pack remaining cupboard contents, linen and towels into boxes (set some aside to use as padding for fragile items in other rooms)
- [ ] **Garage**: pack tools and non-essential items into labelled boxes now that the purge is done

## Week 3 — 14 to 20 July | 2 Weeks Out
**Theme: Main packing push across living areas and study**

See [[Moving/Week 3 Action Plan|Week 3 Action Plan]] for full detail.

### Weekdays (Mon 14 – Fri 18)
- [ ] Coordinate remaining buyer collections (fans, braai, porch bed, light, soda machine — chase anyone who hasn't collected)
- [ ] Intentionally start eating down fridge, freezer, and pantry contents — plan meals around what's there
- [ ] Confirm exact moving day time and details with movers
- [ ] Notify address change: bank, employer, medical aid, insurance — do this now that new address is confirmed

### Weekend (Sat 19 – Sun 20)
- [ ] **Living room**: pack all board games (upright, padded), pack all books into small boxes, disassemble and wrap both bookcases
- [ ] **Gaming area**: pack all peripherals (keyboards, mice, headsets, controllers — bundle cables per device), pack LEGOs (wrap built sets in cling wrap), disassemble and wrap shelf, pack monitors (upright, bubble wrapped) — set aside to transport in car on moving day
- [ ] **Study**: pack 3D printer (secure gantry, remove bed), seal all open filament spools in zip-lock/vacuum bags, pack SBCs (Pi, LattePanda) with their power supplies and SD cards, pack remaining electronics
- [ ] **Main bedroom**: pack out-of-season clothes and shoes, pack dressing table drawers into labelled boxes, wrap and pack side tables
- [ ] **Start filling holes** in rooms that are now cleared — polyfilla, let dry overnight, sand next day

## Week 4 — 21 to 27 July | 1 Week Out
**Theme: Final packing, house restoration, furniture prep**

See [[Moving/Week 4 Action Plan|Week 4 Action Plan]] for full detail.

### Weekdays (Mon 21 – Fri 25)
- [ ] Clear out remaining fridge and freezer — donate, give away, or consume everything perishable
- [ ] Buy polyfilla touch-up paint if not already done
- [ ] Reconfirm movers — confirm time, address, and any access requirements for new place

### Weekend (Sat 26 – Sun 27)

- [ ] **Main bedroom**: pack remaining clothes (leave 4 days of daily wear), disassemble bed frame and bag hardware, wrap headboard
- [ ] **Gaming area**: disassemble desks, bag all hardware — set aside for moving day
- [ ] **Porch**: wrap and pack chair and table set
- [ ] **House restoration**: re-hang all blinds in correct rooms, reattach all removed fixtures (hooks, rails, brackets), finish filling and sanding all remaining holes, touch up paint where needed
- [ ] **Living room**: disassemble TV stand, wrap TV (upright), wrap couch in moving blanket, remove table legs where possible

## Final Push — Thu 30 & Fri 31 July (Take These Days Off)
**Theme: Close everything out, prep for the day**

### Thursday 30 July
- [ ] Pack all remaining kitchen items — only "last morning kit" stays out (kettle, 2 mugs, coffee/tea, spoon)
- [ ] Pack both bathrooms — keep one toiletry bag per person accessible
- [ ] Final wall walkthrough — touch up any remaining holes and paint
- [ ] Confirm all blinds re-hung, all fixtures replaced
- [ ] Disassemble any remaining furniture (TV stand, desks)
- [ ] Set up cat safe room — carrier, blanket, food, water, litter tray, familiar toy

### Friday 31 July
- [ ] Defrost fridge and deep freeze — unplug, leave doors open (24 hrs before move)
- [ ] Pack First Night Box — toiletries, 2 days clothes per person, bedding, chargers, kettle, snacks, toilet paper, documents, medications, cat essentials
- [ ] Pack router, switch, ONT, and all patch cables into a labelled bag — goes in the car
- [ ] Do a full walkthrough of every room — inside every cupboard, under every bed, check walls and ceilings
- [ ] Confirm movers: arrival time, address, access details
- [ ] Charge all devices overnight

## Moving Day — Saturday 1 August

### Morning
- [ ] Cat into safe room early — food, water, litter, door shut, note on door for movers
- [ ] Walk movers through the house and explain the labelling system
- [ ] Direct movers: fragile boxes, what goes in the car vs the truck

### During the Move
- [ ] PCs, monitors, router, and cat travel in **your car** — not the truck
- [ ] Do not feed cat 2–3 hours before travel

### Before Leaving
- [ ] Final walkthrough — every room, every cupboard, garden, porch, garage
- [ ] All keys accounted for (front door, gate, garage, postbox)
- [ ] Handover keys

### At the New Place
- [ ] Set up cat safe room **first** before unloading anything else
- [ ] Set up First Night Box items (kettle, bedding, toiletries)
- [ ] Router up and running
- [ ] Direct movers to place boxes in correct rooms per labels
- [ ] Do not plug in fridge or deep freeze for at least 4 hours after moving
